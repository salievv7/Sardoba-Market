# Sardoba Market

## Project Overview

Sardoba Market is a local marketplace platform designed for Sardoba district.

The platform allows customers to browse products from multiple local shops, reserve items, and contact shop owners quickly.

The goal is to digitize local shopping and make products from every shop accessible in one place.

---

# Main Roles

## Customer

- Browse shops
- Search products
- Reserve products
- Share phone number
- Share current location

---

## Shop Owner

- Create a shop
- Add and edit products
- Update prices
- Manage reservations
- Receive AI suggestions

---

## Admin

- Approve new shops
- Manage users
- Moderate products
- View platform statistics
